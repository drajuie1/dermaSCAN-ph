📚 Learn: MPOX vs Chickenpox

Understand symptoms, spread, and what to do

🦠

MPOX (Monkeypox)

Monkeypox virus · First found in humans 1970

🤒 Symptoms

Swollen lymph nodesHigh fever before rashDeep firm lesionsAll lesions same stageFace → hands → bodyMuscle aches

📡 How It Spreads

  • Close skin-to-skin contact
  • Contact with infected animals
  • Prolonged respiratory contact
  • Contaminated objects

✅ What To Do

  • ISOLATE immediately
  • Call DOH: 1555
  • Cover all lesions
  • Wear a mask always
  • Antiviral treatment available

🚨 See Doctor Immediately If...

  • Difficulty breathing or swallowing
  • Rash spreads to eyes
  • Fever above 39°C
  • Pregnant or immunocompromised
💧

Chickenpox (Varicella)

Varicella-Zoster virus · Very common in children

🤒 Symptoms

Very itchy rashMultiple stages at onceFluid-filled blistersMild fever with rashStarts on chest/back

📡 How It Spreads

  • Airborne — very contagious
  • Direct contact with blisters
  • Contagious 2 days before rash

✅ What To Do

  • Stay home and rest
  • Do NOT scratch blisters
  • Calamine lotion for itch
  • Paracetamol (NOT aspirin)
  • Ask about varicella vaccine

🚨 See Doctor Immediately If...

  • Rash spreads to eyes
  • Rash is red, warm, has pus
  • High fever above 39°C
  • Adult, pregnant, or elderly
